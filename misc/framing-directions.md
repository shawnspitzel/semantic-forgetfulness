## Directions

### Interpretability
    - Frame our research direction more so to align with ongoing interpretability within agentic systems, and form deep connections with neuroscience and memory-related human research. 
### ML Systems
    - We could also frame this paper more in a systems direction. After all, this does involve engineering an OS-like cache witin our model to achieve optimization, and there's a ton of systems questions and endeavors we will go down during this project. One such problem that's relatively open ended is low-latency gist reconstruction during inference. Many related issues will also arise that require unique systems solutions as most of our implementations are inference/deployment-centered.
### Architectural
    - We could also attempt to frame this as an entirely new architecture all together, one that we push for widespread adoption within agentic AI systems. We'd need to frame our paper around a very strong, convincing, & practical argument.
